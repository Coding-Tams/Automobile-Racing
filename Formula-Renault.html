Formula Renault

2009
Brendon Hartley, Edoardo Mortara, Daniel Riccardo, Greg Mansell, Oliver Turvey, Jaime Alguersuari, Max Chilton and Jules Bianchi made their Formula Renault 3.5 debut.
Kevin Magnussen, James Calado and Nico Muller made their Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0 debuts.

2010
Jean-Eric Vergne moved to Formula Renault 3.5 from British Formula 3. Jules Bianchi, Max Chilton and Oliver Turvey left the Formula Renault 3.5 left for GP2, Edoardo Mortara left for Formula 3 Euro Series and Jaime Alguersuari left for Toro Rosso in Formula .
Carlos Sainz Jr, Daniil Kvyat and Rio Haryanto made their Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0.

2011
Alexander Rossi moved to Formula Renault 3.5 from GP3 and Robert Wickens returned to the series from GP3. Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0 winner Kevin Korjus moved to Formula Renault 3.5. Greg Mansell left for the Blancpain Endurance series and Estaban Guerrieri left the series for Indy lights in America.
Formula BMW Europe Champion Robin Frijns entered Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0 series, F4 Eurocup 1.6 champion Stoffel Vandoorne entered the series, Timmy Hansen moved to the series from Formula BMW Europe and Gustavo Menezes and Alex Lynn entered the series.

2012
Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0 champion Robin Frijns moved to Formula Renault 3.5, Jules Bianchi and Sam Bird moved to Formula Renault 3.5 from GP2, Mikhail Aleshin returned to the series from GP2. Nico Muller and Antonio Felix da Costa moved to the series from GP3, Daniel Abt and Kevin Magnussen entered the series. Adrien Tambay and Robert Wickens left the series for DTM series in Germany. Daniel Ricciardo and Jean-Eric Vergne left the series for Toro Rosso in Formula 1.
Esteban Ocon, Oliver Rowland, Jake Dennis, Josh Hill, Alex Albon, Nyck de Vries, Pierre Gasly and Aurelien Panis made their Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0 debut. MP Motorsport changed to Manor Mp Motorsport.

2013
Sergy Sirotkin moved to Formula Renault 3.5 series from Italian Formula 3, Stoffel Vandoorne moved to the series from Formula Renault Eurocup 2.0 and Carlos Sainz Jr returned to the series. Daniel Abt and Sam Bird left the series for GP2 and Kevin Korjus left the series for GP3. Jules Bianchi and Alexander Rossi left the series for Formula 1. Bianchi went to Marussia and Rossi went to Caterham F1 as a reserve driver.
Jack Aitken, Luca Ghiotto and Antonio Fuoco made their Formula Renault Eurocup debut. ART Junnior Team, Euronova and SMP Racing entered the Formula Renault Eurocup.

2014
Pierre Gasly, Oliver Rowland and Luca Ghiotto moved to Formula Renault 3.5 from Formula Renault 2.0. Beitske Visser moved to the series from ADAC Formel Masters, Nicolas Latifi and Esteban Ocon joined the series. Mikhail Aleshin left for Indycar series, Antonio Felix da Costa and Nico Muller left Formula Renault 3.5 for DTM series in Germany. Stoffel Vandoorne left for GP2 and Kevin Magnussen left for Mclaren in Formula 1.
China BRT by Jos and Strakka Racing entered Formula Renault Eurocup. Anthoine Hubert, Charles Leclerc, George Russell, Louis Deletraz and Pietro Fittipaldi made their Formula Renault Eurocup debuts.

2015
Nyck de Vries and Aurelien Panis moved to Formula Renault 3.5 series from Renault 2.0, Sean Gelael moved to the series from Formula 3 and Tom Dillman moved from GP2. Pierre Gasly and Sergy Sirotkin left Formula Renault 3.5 for GP2, Luca Ghiotto left for GP3. ISR Racing and Zeta Corse left Formula Renault 3.5 and Carlin returned to the series.
Dan Ticktum, Ferdinand Habsburg, Jehan Daruvala and Nikita Mazepin made their Formula Renault Eurocup.

2016
Formula V8 3.5 supported many rounds of the International GT Open Championship. Louis Deletraz moved to the series from Renault 2.0 NEC, Pietro Fittipaldi moved to the series from Formula 3. Jack Aitken entered the series, Sean Gelael, Oliver Rowland and Nicolas Latifi left the series for GP2. Nyck de Vries left the series for GP3. DAMS left the series and was replaced by Teo Martin Motorsport, Spirit of Race replaced International Draco Racing and both PONs Racing and Carlin left the series. RP Motorsport and Durango Racing Team entered the series. Lando Norris, Robert Shwartzman and Marcus Armstrong made their Formula Renault Eurocup debut.S

2018
The Formula Renault Eurocup supported many rounds of the Blancpain GT series and some rounds of the European Le Mans series.
