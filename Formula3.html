2003 Formula 3 Euro Series Season

Ryan Briscoe won the championship.

Ryan Briscoe moved to Formula 3 Euro Series from German Formula 3 and International Formula 3000. Timo Glock and Markus Winkelhock moved to Formula 3 Euro Series from German Formula 3.

Adam Carroll moved to Formula 3 from British Formula 3 National Class.

Christian Klein and Bruno Spengler moved from Formula Renault 2.0 Germany, Spengler had also competed in Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0. Robert Kubica moved to Formula 3 Euro Series from Eurocup Formula Reanault 2.0 and Formula Renault Italy.

Jamie Green moved to Formula 3 Euro Series from Formula Renault 2.0 Uk, Lucas Di Grassi moved from Formula Reanault 2.0 Brazil.

Nic Rosberg moved to Formula 3 Euro Series from Formula BMW ADAC.

2004 Formula 3 Euro Series Season

Jamie Green won the championship, Franck Perera won the Rookie Cup.

Loic Duval moved to Formula 3 Euro Series from Championnat de France Formula Reanault 2.0. Giedo Van der Garde moved to Formula 3 Euro Series from Formula Renault 2000.

Lewis Hamilton moved to Formula 3 Euro Series from Formula Reanault 2.0 UK.

Adam Carroll and Lucas di Grassi left Formula 3 Euro Series for British Formula 3, Markus Winkelhock left Formula 3 for German Formula 3.

Ryan Briscoe, Timo Glock and Christian Klein left Formula 3 Euro Series for Formula 1, Klein joined Jaguar Racing, Glock joined Jordan and Briscoe joined Panasonic Toyota as a Test driver.
