Formula 3

European Formula 3 1975-1984

Formula 3 was a one race event for national teams from 1966 untl 1975.

In 1975 the series became FIA European Formula 3 Cup.

In 1985 the European Formula 3 Championship was replaced by European Formula 3 until 1990.
