2005 GP2 Season

Nico Rosberg won the championship.

GP2 replaced Formula 3000, some of the teams that competed in Formula 3000 entered GP2. The teams were Durango, Arden International, Coloni Motorsport, DAMS, Super Nova Racing and BCN Competicion. Some new teams entered the series, they were iSport International, Hitech Piquet Racing, ART Grand Prix, David Price Racing, Campos Racing and Racing Engineering.

Gianmaria Bruni and Giorgio Pantano moved to GP2 from Formula 1, Adam Carroll and Nelson Piquet moved to GP2 from British Formula 3. Heikki Kovalainen moved to GP2 from World Series by Nissan, Neel Jani and Ryan Sharp moved from Formula Renault V6 Eurocup, Scott Speed moved from Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0. Nico Rosberg moved from Formula 3 Euroseries to GP2.

2004 Formula 3000 champion Vitantonio Liuzzi had moved to Red Bull Racing in Formula 1.

2006 GP2 Season

2010 GP2 Season

Pastor Maldonado won the championship. Rapox were team champions

Gp2 now had a feeder with the formation of GP3.

Ho-Pin Tung returned to the series after a season in Superleague Formula. Charles Pic, Oliver Turvey and Adrian Zaugg moved to the series from Formula Renault 3.5 series.

Formula 3 Euro Series champion Jules Bianchi and Sam Bird moved to GP2 from Formula 3 Euro Series. Max Chilton moved to Gp2 from British Formula 3, All-Japan Formula Three champion Marcus Ericsson moved to Gp2 and Brendon Hartley made his debut.

Edoardo Mortara went back to Formula 3 and Romain Grosjean returned to GP2.
