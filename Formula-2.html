Formula 2 was codified in 1948, the first race was at the Stockholm Grand Prix at Skarpnack Airfield, which was held according to the new Formula 2 rules.

1970 Formula 2 Season

Clay Regazzoni won the Championship.

Ronnie Peterson, Patrick Depailler, Emerson Fittipaldi, Carlos Reutemann and Tetsu Ikuzawa made their Formula 2 debut.

Emerson Fittipaldi was the first Brazilian in Formula 2, Carlos Reutemann was the first Argentinian and Tetsu Ikuzawa the first Japanese driver. The graded drivers during the seson were Jackie Stewart, Jack Brabham, Rolf Stommelen, Jochen Rindt, Graham Hill, Jo Siffert, Jackie Ickx, Chris Amon and Jean-Pierre Beltoise.

Formula 2 became Formula 3000 in 1985 until 2004 when it became GP2. In 2017 the series became Formula 2 again.

2017 Formula 2 Season

Charles Leclerc won the championship and Russian Time were the team champions.

GP2 was replaced with Formula 2.

Nyck De Vries moved to Formula 2 from GP3, Sergio Sette Camara moved to the series from European Formula 3.

Arthur Pic left Formula 2, Raffaele Marciello left Formula 2 for GT Racing.

Callum Ilott and Lando Norris made their Formula 2 debuts, Ilott replaced the injured Johnny Cecotto and Norris replace Ralph Boschung for the Abu Dahbi Grand Prix.

Mitch Evans and Alex Lynnn left Fomrula 2 for Formula E, Evans went to new team Jaguar Racing and Alex Lynn became a reserve driver.

Pierre Gasly left Formula 2 for the Super Formula Championship in Japan and later joined Toro Rosso in Formula 1 from the Malaysian Grand Prix. Antonio Giovinazzi and Sergey Sirotkin left for Formula 1, Giovinazzi became the Ferrari test driver, Sirotkin became the Renault test and reserve driver. Sergey Sirotkin returned to Formula 2 to replace the injured Alexander Albon at Baku.

Carlin withdrew from the series to concentrate on Indy Lights in America.

2018 Formula 2 Season

George Russell won the championship and Carlin were the team champions.

GP3 champion George Russell moved to Formula 2.

Maximilian Gunther and Lando Norris moved to Formula 2 from Formula 3, Dan Ticktum replaced Gunther at BWT Arden for the Abu Dhabi round as Gunther left for Formula E. Santino Ferrucci moved to Formula 2, he was banned for two rounds due to multiple violations including deliberately crashing into Arjun Maini. He was later fired by Trident and Alessio Lorandi replaced him.

Nobuharu Matsushita left Formula 2 for the Super Formula Championship in Japan, Oliver Rowland left for the World Endurance Championship. Charles Leclerc left for Sauber in Formula 1.

Carlin returned to Formula 2 and Charouz Racing entered Formula 2. Racing Engineering was on the entry list but later withdrew to focus on the Le Mans Series.

2019 Formula 2 Season

Nyck de Vries won the championship and DAMS werre the team champions.

DAMS won their first team title in Formula 2.

Nabouharu Matsushita returned to Formula 2, Anthoine Hubert, Tatiana Calderon, Giuliano Alesi and Nikita Mazepin moved to Formula 2 from GP3. Guanyu Zhou and Mick Schumacher moved to Formula 2 from Formula 3.

Callum Ilott, Juan Manuel Correra and Mahaveer Raghunathan joined Formula 2.

Antonio Fuoco left Formula 2 to become Ferrari test driver, Maximilian Gunther left for Formula E. Lando Norris, Alex Albon and George Russell left for Formula 1, Norris went to Mclaren, Albon went to Toro Rosso and Russell to Williams.

Patricio O'Ward replaced Raghunathan at the Red Bull Ring due to Mahaveer Raghunathan being banned for collecting 12 penalty points on his racing licence.

Russian Time withdrew from the championship, their entry and assets were sold to Virtuosi Racing, the team entered as UNI-Virtuosi Racing. Arden International entered into a partnership with HWA Racelab, Charouz Racing System formed a partnership with Sauber Motorsports.

Anthoine Hubert, Juan Manuel Correra and Giuliano alesi were involved in a fatal crash during the race at Spa-Francorchamps, Hubert was killed and Correra was seriously injured. Alesi's car was impounded as part of the investigation into the accident. At the following race at Monza BWT Arden and Charouz and Trident only ran one car each.
