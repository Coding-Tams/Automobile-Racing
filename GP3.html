2010 GP3 Season

Esteban Gutierrez won the championship

The first season of GP3, it was the feeder series for GP2, each of the teams ran three cars each.

Esteban Gutierrez drove for ART Grand Prix in Formula 3 Euro Series and became ART Grand Prix driver in GP3. His team mate American Alexander Rossi joined from International Formula Masters.

Formula BMW Pacific champion Rio Haryanto joined GP3, Swiss Formula Renault 2.0 champion Nico Muller joined GP3. Adrien Tambay, Antonio Felix da Costa and Jean-Eric Vergne competed in GP3 for just two rounds of the championship.

Former Formula 1 driver Ralf Schumacher partnered with Peter Mucke at Mucke Motorsport to form RSC Mucke Motorsport.

2011 GP3 Season

Valtteri Bottas won the championship.

Valtteri Bottas and Alexander Sima had moved to GP3 from Formula 3, James Calado moved to the series from British Formula 3 and Mitch Evans moved to GP3 from Australian Formula 3. German Formula 3 champion Tom Dillman only raced at the first round of the season. Antonio Felix da Costa moved to GP3 for the full season.

Alexander Rossi and Robert Wickens left GP3 for Formula Renault 3.5 Series

2012 GP3 Season

Mitch Evans won the championship.

Three women competed in the GP3 during the season, Carmen Jorda, Alice Powell and Vicky Piria.

Mucke Motorsport did not compete in the 2012 season and the Addox team left the series, they were replaced by Trident and Ocean Racing Technology replaced Tech 1 Racing.

2013 GP3 Season

Daniil Kvyat won the championship and ART Grand Prix were the team champions.

Daniil Kvyat moved to GP3 from Formula Renault 2.0 Alps, Carlos Sainz Jr moved to the series from European Formula 3 and Alexander Sims made his debut.

Daniel Abt and Mitch Evans moved to GP2, Antonio Felix da Costa moved to Formula Renault 3.5

Lotus GP reverted back to ART Grand Prix and Mucke Motorsport withdrew from the series. Bamboo Engineering replaced Atech CRS Grand Prix and Koiranen racing replaced Ocean Racing Technology.

2014 GP3 Season

Alex Lynn won the championship and Carlin were the team champions.

It was the only season that ART Grand Prix did not win the team championship.

Alex Lynn and Jann Mardenbrough moved to GP3 from European Formula 3, Beitske Visser moved from ADAC Formel Masters to GP3.

Carlos Sainz Jr left for Formula Renault 3.5 Series and Alexander Sims left for British GT Championship.

Daniil Kvyat left for Toro Rosso in Formula 1.

MW Arden changed reverted back to Arden International, Bamboo Engineering left the series and was replaced by Hilmer Motorsport. Marussia Manor Racing pulled out of the Sochi round due to financial trouble.

2016 GP3 Season

Charles Leclerc won the championship and ART Grand Prix were the team champions.

The Teams were allowed to enter four cars each.

Giuliano Alesi, son of former Formula 1 driver Jean ALesi, moved to GP3 from French Formula 4.

Alex ALbon, Jake Dennis, Tatiana Calderon and Mahaveer Raghunathan moved to GP£ from Formula 3. Nyck de Vries moved from Formula Renault 3.5 series to GP3 and Jack Aitken moved to GP3 from Eurocup Formula Renault 2.0.

Luca Ghiotto left for GP2, Jann Mardenbrough left for Super GT and All-Japan F3 and Esteban Ocon left for DTM.

Carlin and Status Grand Prix left the series due to lack of sponsorship and were replaced by DAMS. Virtuosi Racing were scheduled to enter the series but were removed from the entry list before the season.
