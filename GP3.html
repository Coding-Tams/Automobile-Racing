2010 GP3 Season

Esteban Gutierrez won the championship

The first season of GP3, it was the feeder series for GP2, each of the teams ran three cars each.

Esteban Gutierrez drove for ART Grand Prix in Formula 3 Euro Series and became ART Grand Prix driver in GP3. His team mate American Alexander Rossi joined from International Formula Masters.

Formula BMW Pacific champion Rio Haryanto joined GP3, Swiss Formula Renault 2.0 champion Nico Muller joined GP3. Adrien Tambay, Antonio Felix da Costa and Jean-Eric Vergne competed in GP3 for just two rounds of the championship.

Former Formula 1 driver Ralf Schumacher partnered with Peter Mucke at Mucke Motorsport to form RSC Mucke Motorsport.

2011 GP3 Season

Valtteri Bottas won the championship.

Valtteri Bottas and Alexander Sima had moved to GP3 from Formula 3, James Calado moved to the series from British Formula 3 and Mitch Evans moved to GP3 from Australian Formula 3. German Formula 3 champion Tom Dillman only raced at the first round of the season. Antonio Felix da Costa moved to GP3 for the full season.

Alexander Rossi and Robert Wickens left GP3 for Formula Renault 3.5 Series

2012 GP3 Season

Mitch Evans won the championship.

Three women competed in the GP3 during the season, Carmen Jorda, Alice Powell and Vicky Piria.

Mucke Motorsport did not compete in the 2012 season and the Addox team left the series, they were replaced by Trident and Ocean Racing Technology replaced Tech 1 Racing.
